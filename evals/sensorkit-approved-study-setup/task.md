# SensorKit Study Setup Review

## Problem/Feature Description

A university sleep research team is preparing an iPhone and Apple Watch study
app that will collect ambient light, recorded motion, wrist temperature, ECG,
and PPG signals. Their draft setup plan says the app can use automatic signing,
only needs a broad privacy prompt, and can begin fetching data immediately after
participants approve the study sensors.

## Output Specification

Create `sensorkit-study-setup-review.md` with a correction-focused setup
review. Cover app capabilities, signing/provisioning, authorization,
participant-facing privacy copy, sensor-specific configuration, and timing
constraints that affect the first data export.
